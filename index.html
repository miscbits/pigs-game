The Rules

Pigs is an easy game to play. Two players take turns rolling a die

If a player rolls anything but a one, that score is put into a bank

That player can risk the score and roll the die again to get more points

turns end when a player decides to add the points to their bank or if they roll a 1

If a player rolls a 1, they lose all of their at risk points

Each player can roll up to three times per turn

Once a player hits 50 points, the game ends and that player wins!
